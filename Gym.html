Why is it important to do a progressive overload?


For the muscles to grow and get stronger you have to stimulate the muscle enough,you can do this by having a low RIR(repetitions in reserve) therefore if you do ten reps with a RIR of two you could do two more reps but you leave them in RIR.
This is called PROGRESIVE OVERLOAD.
We can help you with your progressive overload by tracking it and giving you advise
